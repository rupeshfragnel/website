Our Heritage

Founded in 1910, Christou has been a family-owned business dedicated to producing the finest quality olive products from the sun-drenched groves of Greece. For over a century, we have maintained the traditional methods of cultivation and production while embracing innovation to ensure exceptional quality.

Our olives are hand-picked at the optimal moment of ripeness and processed within hours to preserve their distinctive flavor and nutritional value. We take pride in our sustainable farming practices and commitment to environmental stewardship.
